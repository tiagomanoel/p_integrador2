Projeto Integrador - UNIVESP

Sobre o Projeto

The UNIVESP Integrative Project is an initiative aimed at uniting theory and practice, providing students with the opportunity to develop real and applicable projects for the job market. This project seeks to integrate knowledge acquired throughout the course, developing essential skills for the students' professional future.

Project Objectives:

  • Integrate theoretical and practical knowledge.
  • Develop teamwork skills.
  • Prepare students for market challenges.
